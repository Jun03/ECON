WHAT   IS   E-CON ?



E-CON , synopsis for "ENGINEER'S CONNECTION" is a platform for connecting the students together for - sharing information & ideas, discussing about various topics, posting, etc.

E-CON is basically developed for students of AIMT (Ambalika Institute of Management & Technology). E-CON helps you to connect yourself with your colleagues, classmates and friends. It also helps you to interact with your seniors and juniors.

The advantage of being a part of E-CON is that you dont need any other social media website to get in touch with your college-mates. E-CON provides a secure and private environment for the students. It is a perfect platform for bringing all the Ambalikans together. There are a number of purposes for which you can rely on E-CON.
